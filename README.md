# Revenue and Profitability Drivers in Retail Sales

## Business Context
Understanding what drives revenue and profitability is critical for financial planning and decision-making. This project analyzes retail transaction data to identify key drivers of financial performance across products, regions, and time.

## Key Questions
- What are the primary drivers of revenue and profit?
- How do profit margins vary across product categories and regions?
- How has revenue and profitability evolved over time?

## Data
This analysis uses a publicly available retail sales dataset (~10,000 transactions) containing order-level information such as sales, profit, product categories, regions, and order dates.

## Tools Used
- Excel (exploratory analysis, KPI validation)
- SQL (data aggregation and metric calculation)
- Tableau (visualization and dashboarding)

## Methodology (High-level)
- Performed exploratory analysis to understand overall revenue and profit distribution
- Calculated core financial KPIs (revenue, profit, margin)
- Analyzed performance trends over time and across dimensions
- Summarized insights with a focus on actionable business implications
